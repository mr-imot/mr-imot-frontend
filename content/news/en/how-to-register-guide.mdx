## Step 1: Open the Registration Form

[Registration link](/en/register?type=developer)

## Step 2: Fill in Your Company Data

Required information:

- **Contact Person** - your name or the responsible person's name
- **Phone** - for direct contact with buyers
- **Email Address** - for confirmation and notifications
- **Office Address** - you can mark the exact location on the map
- **Website** (optional, but recommended) - if you don't have a website, [Prodigy Corp](https://www.prodigycorp.io/) can create a professional website at a preferential price

## Step 3: Confirm Your Email

After clicking "Create Profile", you will receive a confirmation email.

⚠️ Don't see it? Check your "Spam" folder or [contact us](/contact).

## Step 4: Wait for Verification

**IMPORTANT:** Full access requires a brief verification by our team (usually within 24 hours). This ensures the platform remains reliable and accessible only to real developers and investors.

After approval, you will be able to:

- Manage your projects
- Add and edit listings
- Track statistics and interest in your properties
- Communicate directly with buyers
