We’ve added full Russian language support across Mister Imot—news, listings, dashboards, and navigation. Добро пожаловать!

## What’s new

- Russian UI across desktop and mobile.
- News and articles localized, with hreflang and SEO ready.
- Developer dashboards and flows translated.

## If you spot anything

- Found a typo or odd phrasing? Drop us a note via [Contact](/contact).  
- Prefer to message us on socials? We’re on LinkedIn, Facebook, and Instagram (@MisterImot).

## For developers

- If you’re a real estate developer, register your project directly: [List your project](/register?type=developer).
- Already registered? Your dashboard also supports Russian—log in and keep your listings fresh.

- More localized content and guides.

- More languages on the way—stay tuned.
